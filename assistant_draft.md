## How Generative AI Can Advance Business Analytics

- **Automated report generation**: Generative AI can analyze large datasets and automatically produce executive summaries, narrative reports, and visualizations that would traditionally require significant analyst time, enabling faster decision-making cycles.
- **Natural language querying of business data**: Non-technical stakeholders can use conversational interfaces powered by large language models to query databases and receive instant, human-readable insights without needing to write SQL or understand underlying data structures.
- **Predictive scenario simulation**: Generative AI can model and simulate various business scenarios based on historical trends, helping product managers and executives explore "what-if" situations and anticipate outcomes before committing resources.
